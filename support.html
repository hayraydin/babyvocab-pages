Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I start and stop recording?

On the app's main screen, just tap the big Microphone icon to begin your recording session. When you're ready to finish, tap the Stop icon that appears while recording.

2. Is my data private? Do I need an account?

Your privacy is important! BabyVocab doesn't require any sign-in or account. All your information—like daily progress, word insights, achievements, and your baby's age—is stored securely right on your own device. We don't keep your personal recording data on our servers.

3. What are AI Suggestions for?

Our AI Suggestions offer gentle, friendly ideas tailored to your baby's age and the words you've used recently:

  • Daily Tip: A sweet, actionable idea to help nurture your baby's communication, thinking skills, or the loving bond you share.
  • New Word Ideas: Fun, alternative words or phrases to help you playfully add more variety to the language your baby hears.

For these AI features to sprinkle their magic, your device will need an internet connection.

4. Why does BabyVocab ask for microphone permission?

We need microphone access so the app can hear you during your recording sessions! This is how BabyVocab helps you keep track of the words you share with your baby and offers personalized insights and suggestions.

5. What if the word counts or insights seem a bit off?

Sometimes, things like background noise can make speech recognition a little tricky. BabyVocab aims to be a helpful guide, but please remember the loving interaction itself is what matters most! If you have specific concerns, we're here to help. Also, ensure your device is connected to the internet when checking AI features like suggestions or word categorization, as they need it to work.

6. How can I change my baby's age in the app?

You can easily update your baby's age on the app's main Home screen. You'll usually find the age selector near the top. Keeping the age current helps us provide the most relevant AI suggestions for your little one's stage.
